Introduction

Welcome to QuickChat, your ultimate WhatsApp companion. Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we handle your information when you use our app. We are committed to transparency and protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

QuickChat follows a privacy-first approach and collects minimal information:

  • Phone Numbers: When you enter a phone number to open it in WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business.
  • Contact Information: When you voluntarily save phone numbers within the app's contact manager.
  • Usage Data: App activity logs stored locally for your reference (call history, timestamps).
  • App Preferences: Your settings such as lock screen preferences, theme choices, and display options.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your personal messages or chat content
  • Contact lists from your phone
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Payment information

How We Use Your Information

All collected information serves specific purposes within the app:

  • To enable direct WhatsApp access without saving numbers to device contacts
  • To maintain your personal contact list within the app
  • To provide WhatsApp Web QR code scanning functionality
  • To display your recent activity history for easy reference
  • To implement security features like app locking
  • To improve app performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security Local Only

Local Storage

All your data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not transmit any information to external servers or third-party services. Your contacts, history, and preferences remain private and secure on your smartphone.

Security Measures

  • Device Encryption: Data is stored using your device's native encryption
  • Lock Screen Protection: Optional PIN, pattern, or biometric authentication
  • No Internet Transmission: Zero data sent to our servers
  • Secure Deletion: Complete data removal upon app uninstallation

Sharing Your Information

We do not share, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone.

The only exception is when you explicitly choose to share contacts or messages through your device's sharing capabilities (WhatsApp, email, messaging apps, etc.). This sharing is controlled entirely by you.

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Full Access: View, edit, or delete any contact you've saved
  • History Management: Clear your recent activity logs at any time
  • Privacy Settings: Enable/disable app locking and other security features
  • Data Export: Share your saved contacts via standard sharing options
  • Complete Removal: Uninstall the app to delete all associated data
